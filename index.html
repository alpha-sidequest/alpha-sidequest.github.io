Aircraft Fleet

F-35A Lightning II

Role: 5th-generation stealth multirole fighter

Code meaning: F = Fighter, 35 = design number, A = Australian variant

Crew: 1 pilot

Bases: Williamtown (main), Tindal

Squadrons: No. 3, 77, 2 OCU (Williamtown), No. 75 (Tindal)

Key Capabilities: AN/APG-81 AESA (Active Electronically Scanned Array) radar, 360° DAS (Distributed Aperture System) sensors, stealth design, sensor fusion, internal weapons bay. Mach 1.6, excellent manoeuvrability.

F/A-18F Super Hornet

Role: Multirole strike fighter

Crew: 2 (pilot + weapons systems officer)

Base: Amberley

Squadron: No. 1 Squadron

EA-18G Growler

Role: Electronic Attack / Jamming (SEAD - Suppression of Enemy Air Defences)

Crew: 2

Base/Squadron: Amberley, No. 6 Squadron

E-7A Wedgetail

Role: AEW&C (Airborne Early Warning & Control)

Crew: 6–10 (flight + mission crew)

Base/Squadron: Williamtown, No. 2 Squadron

P-8A Poseidon

Role: Maritime Patrol / ASW (Anti-Submarine Warfare)

Crew: 9–11

Base: Edinburgh

Squadrons: No. 11 & 292

C-17A Globemaster III

Role: Strategic heavy lift transport

Crew: 3 (2 pilots + loadmaster)

Base/Squadron: Amberley, No. 36 Squadron

KC-30A Multi-Role Tanker Transport

Role: Air-to-air refuelling + strategic transport

Crew: 3–5

Base/Squadron: Amberley, No. 33 Squadron
