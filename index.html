F-35A Lightning II

Role: 5th-generation stealth multirole fighter

In Service: 72 | Bases: Williamtown (main), Tindal | Squadrons: No. 3, 77, 2 OCU, No. 75

  • Engines: 1 × Pratt & Whitney F135 (afterburning turbofan) — provides ~43,000 lbs thrust with afterburner, excellent power-to-weight ratio.
  • AN/APG-81 AESA Radar (Northrop Grumman): Active Electronically Scanned Array radar. Allows simultaneous tracking of many targets, high-resolution mapping, and electronic warfare functions.
  • DAS (Distributed Aperture System): 360° infrared cameras around the aircraft. Gives the pilot "see-through" vision — can look "through" the floor or behind the plane via helmet display.
  • Sensor Fusion: Combines data from all sensors into one clear picture for the pilot.
  • Weapons: Internal bays (stealthy) for AIM-120 AMRAAM (long-range air-to-air), AIM-9X Sidewinder (short-range), JDAM smart bombs. External hardpoints when stealth not needed.

F/A-18F Super Hornet

Role: Multirole strike fighter

In Service: 24 | Base: Amberley | Squadron: No. 1

  • Engines: 2 × General Electric F414 — reliable, powerful turbofans.
  • APG-79 AESA Radar: Advanced fire-control radar for air-to-air and air-to-ground targeting.
  • Weapons: AIM-120, AIM-9X, Harpoon anti-ship missiles, JDAM bombs, 20mm cannon.
